What mathematics is all about

In short, informal words, Mathematics is the language we use to describe and understand patterns, solve puzzles, and make sense of the universe. It’s the science of possibilities, structure, order and relation, relevant to various fields such as Engineering, Science, Music and everyday life. Counting, shopping, budgeting, time management and technology amongst others, are very evident applications of mathematics.

Some Simple Mathematical Terms

Term What it means
Basic Number A symbol representing quantity (e.g., 0, 1, 2… or words like “seven”).
Fraction A way to represent parts of a whole number or integer, like ½ or ¾.
Integer A whole number, which can be positive, negative, or zero (e.g., -3, 0, 4).
Advanced Variable A symbol (often a letter) that represents an unknown value in equations or expressions, like x or y.
Function A relation where each input (x) has exactly one output (y), like y = 2x + 3.
Other Terms: probablity, equation, funtion, median, mode, matrix, vector, etc...
